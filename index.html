Hi, I'm Michelle!

the designer - me!

Hey there! I'm a junior at the University of Michigan majoring in User Experience Design with a minor in German. I originally hail (ha!) from Ann Arbor, so I didn't have to travel very far to get to college. Nowadays, I goes home to do laundry and to catch up with my yellow lab, Tesla. I have three siblings, so I never had a dull moment growing up- I definitely didn't mind, though! There was always an opportunity to do something with one of my sisters or brother, and I don't know what I would do without them.

Tesla, my dog, loves walks and regularly bonks her head on the wall while playing fetch.

This semester, I am excited to have been able to take a Web Design, a Web Applications, and a Graphic Design course. Only in the past few years did I discover an interest in design. I've had a lot of fun learning the ropes of typography, color, accessibility, and conveying meaning through visuals. Outside of class, I am heavily involved in the University of Michigan Women's Glee Club (WGC). For the past 2 years, I have been a section leader for the Alto 1s (second lowest treble voice part), and this past year I picked up the job of designing merch for Club. Next year, I will take over the position of Concert Manager, and will be in charge of planning the concert for each semester (hopefully, I'll be able to sing in another real concert by the time I graduate). I'm also a member of WGC's a capella subset group, Midnight Blue. Our virtual concert will soon be posted online!
